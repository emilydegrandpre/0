About emily deGrandpre

Studying Architeture at CMU greatly influences my way of life and thinking about some of the problems humanity faces and will face.

The work here inspires me to maintain a positive attitude and dedication towards developing stronger analytical, interpersonal, techinal, and creative skills. I seek design learning in order to discover both the practical and imagined and the unimagined.

When I'm not working in studio or learning about new people, places, and philosophies, I incorporate my passion for health and fitness in my life through long distance running, dance performance, cooking and eating. In my leisure you could find me reading, buying concert tickets, reorganizing the apps on my phone and the things on my desk, or visiting the theatre for fine art, dance performance, and lectures.
