About emily deGrandpre

Studying Architeture at CMU greatly influences my way of life and thinking about some of the challenges that we face as designers, members of the community, and as individuals.

The work here inspires me to maintain a positive attitude and dedication towards developing stronger analytical, interpersonal, techinal, and creative skills.

When I'm not working in studio or learning about new people, places, and philosophies, I incorporate my passion for health and fitness in my life through running, dance performance, cooking, and enjoying meals with my family or friends. In my leisure you could find me reading or journaling, buying concert tickets, reorganizing the apps on my phone and the things on my desk, or visiting a theatre or museum for fine art, dance performance or musicals and whichever lectures I can get to.
