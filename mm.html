Marble Mover

This project relied strictly on the materiality of 11x17" paper to create a structure on or through which a marble could travel. I used the curve of a scanned tree branch to create a path that the marble was intended to move along. Working extensively with different types of oragami and kirigami enabled me to think about symmetry, rhythm, direction of a marble's movement and speed dependent on gravity, limits, and testing my iterations.

Three images show my concept, my process work, and a hybrid image using both an analog draft and digital media.
