Digital Media II

Folly
Organic Modeling and Panel Tool in Rhino

This assignment required the use of paneling tools to create a "fruit or vegetable inspired folly" for the outside of Hunt Library on Carnegie Mellon's Campus. My 1st phase focused on the modeling process of an avocado and the 2nd phase focused on the process of creating a form derived from the avocado and paneling over it using custom 3D paneling tools.
